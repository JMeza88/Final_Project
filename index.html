A little bit about me:

Welcome to my online portfolio! I am a bilingual Social Studies teacher currently teaching bilingual U.S. History to native Spanish-speakers and grade 9th Global History at Mineola High School. I also have experience as a Department Coordinator and 9th grade Social Studies teacher for three years as MESA Charter High School in Bushwick, Brooklyn.
Read More

My goal as a Social Studies teacher to provide rigorous but accessible instruction that prepares students for the ever-changing required Regents Examinations, but also to instill a sense of accountability, self-efficacy and success in my students by using a variety of learning styles and teaching methods to engage all students. Regardless of the individual challenges my students have as learners, my philosophy is to always maintain clear and high expectations.

At Mineola High School, I now have the unique opportunity of working in an Apple Certified School District and learning new ways to teach my students using iPad technology. It has created a learning curve for myself while also providing a multitude of unique ways to engage my students through the use of interactive apps such as Padlet, Edmodo and Pear Deck. I am continuing to grow as a teacher with less than 5 years experience. I am ecstatic to be part of a such a highly dynamic and tech-advanced school while being an advocate for my bilingual students both inside and outside of the classroom. Read Less
